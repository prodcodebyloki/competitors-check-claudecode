## Pricing Model

- **API:** Pay-per-token (input + output billed separately)
- **Claude.ai:** Subscription (monthly or annual)
- **Annual Discount:** Available on Pro and Teams plans
- **Free Tier:** API has no free tier (credits for new accounts only)

## Positioning

Mid-to-premium pricing. Haiku is cost-competitive for high-volume tasks. Opus is premium, positioned against GPT-4o and Gemini Ultra. We compete on quality and safety, not lowest price.

## Competitive Pricing Notes

- Cheaper than OpenAI GPT-4o on input tokens at Sonnet tier
- Mistral is significantly cheaper at the commodity end
- Google Gemini Flash undercuts Haiku on price — volume-sensitive customers may prefer it
