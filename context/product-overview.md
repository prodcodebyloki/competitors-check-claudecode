# Anthropic — Product Overview

## What We Do

Anthropic is an AI safety company that builds Claude — a family of large language models designed to be safe, accurate, and helpful. We serve developers, enterprises, and consumers who need reliable AI assistants for reasoning, coding, writing, and analysis.

## Vision

Build AI systems that are safe and beneficial for humanity. We pursue frontier AI research while shipping products that demonstrate safety and capability are complementary, not opposing.

## Target Market

- **Primary:** Developers and enterprises integrating AI via API (Claude API)
- **Secondary:** Knowledge workers and teams via Claude.ai (consumer + Teams plans)
- **Vertical focus:** Legal, finance, coding, customer support, research

## Key Value Propositions

1. Safety-first design — Constitutional AI and RLHF reduce harmful outputs
2. Long context window — up to 200K tokens for large document analysis
3. Strong reasoning and instruction-following vs peers
4. Enterprise-grade reliability, compliance (SOC 2, HIPAA-eligible)

## Current Stage

Well-funded frontier AI lab (Series C+). Competing directly with OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, and Mistral.

## Differentiators vs Market

- Constitutional AI methodology — unique safety training approach
- Best-in-class long context handling
- Strong performance on nuanced reasoning and coding tasks
- Smaller, more focused team vs Google/Meta — faster iteration on core models
